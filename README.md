# Training Materials for Risk Return Trade-off Analysis

##   Contents

There are three folders: 2022, 2024 and 2025. The 2022 folder contains a lecture and materials on spatial risk assessment that was delivered to CSA course in 2022. The 2024 corresponds to the 2024 risk-return CA lecture and materials. The 2025 folder has spatial bioeconomic modeling lecture slides delivered online to LUANAR postgraduate students
